Painting - What is it?

Home All About Art Art Forms Architecture Painting

Painting, like music, is the expression of different ideas and emotions. It's a creation that is viewed with certain aesthetic qualities to make it pleasing to look at. It is to show different ideas in a 2-dimensional visual language.

You might be thinking, 'what is the importance of painting?' It's to present new and different ideas and it can even be people viewing the world from a different perspective.

Interesting facts about painting!

  • People around the world have been painting for as long as 30,000 years!
  • Spray paint was invented in 1949, that means that people have only been using spray paint for 73 years!

    • Painting is a task that can be both calming and relaxing and even something you could do in your free time. It's a way to let your mind wander and see what it can come up with.

    What is watercolour?

    Water colour is really just a painting method. When the paints are made pigments, they suspend in the water-base solution you are using (preferably water). There are a few different types of water colours such as:

  • gouache
  • paint
  • acrylic
  • chalk
