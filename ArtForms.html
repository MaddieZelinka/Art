There are 7 major art forms that are commonly used all around the world. These are:

  • Painting
  • Sculpting
  • Architecture
  • Literature
  • Music
  • Cinema
  • Theatre

    • These are not what some people may classify as art such as painting, but painting, drawing and sketching aren't the only types of art there are.

    Music is a different type of art that combines vocal or instrumental sounds to form beauty or emotional expression. Music even helps us feel emotions about all the different experiences in our lives.
