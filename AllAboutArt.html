Art Supplies!

The first art supply is prismacolors. Prismacolors are a brand of professional art supplies that originated in 1938 by the Eagle Pencil Company which was then taken over by Berol. It is currently being manufactured by Newell Brands.

Prismacolour pencils are known for their light-resistant pigment that ensure a smooth and rich colour saturation. Even though they are expensive, they have their pros such as:

  • easy to blend with
  • doesn't wear down easily
  • break-resistant
  • high quality

    • Why Are Some Art Supplies So Expensive?

    A lot of art supplies that you would find at a general art store would be expensive because when the creators of the item choose a price to sell, they have to factor in how much it costs to make the item, and how to make a profit. There are many ways that you can save money on your art supplies such as:

  • Coupon apps
  • Reusing your materials
  • Looking after your art supplies so you won't feel the need to get new ones
  • Don't make impulse purchases
